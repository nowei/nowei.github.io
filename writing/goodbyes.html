Goodbyes

Goodbyes are weird

    I graduated from undergrad in spring 2019 and as I was sitting there in the stadium where the graduation was held, a thought came up. Everyone is going off to do great and amazing things, with their whole lives ahead of them. But the question that stuck in mind was, will I ever see any of these people again?

    We get exposed to a lot of people in college and after college, everyone goes kind of goes their separate ways.
